# Cacao Flavor Profiling App - User Guide

Welcome to the Cacao Flavor Profiling App! This tool is designed to help you digitally record, visualize, and compare cacao sample evaluations based on the **Cacao of Excellence** standards.

## 1. Starting a New Evaluation
1.  **Open the App**: Identify the "New Evaluation" button on the home page or navigation bar.
2.  **Enter Sample Details**: Fill in the required fields:
    *   **Sample Code**: Unique ID for your sample.
    *   **Session Date & Time**: Automatically filled, but adjustable.
    *   **Evaluator Name**: Your name or ID.
    *   **Type**: Choose between "Cacao Mass" or "Chocolate".
3.  **Start Grading**: Click "Start Evaluation" to unlock the attribute sliders.

## 2. Scoring Attributes
1.  **Rate Intensities**: Use the sliders (0-10) to rate each flavor attribute (e.g., Cocoa, Acidity, Bitterness).
    *   **Sub-attributes**: Expand a category (like "Fresh Fruit") to rate specific notes (e.g., "Berry", "Citrus").
2.  **Global Quality**: Assign a global quality score at the bottom of the form.
3.  **Mobile Navigation**:
    *   Use the floating menu button (bottom-right) to quickly jump to specific attributes.
    *   Tap the **Top** button in the menu to return to the sample details.
    *   **Sliders**: The application features touch-safe sliders to prevent accidental changes while scrolling.

## 3. Saving & Exporting
1.  **Download PDF**: Once finished, click "Download PDF" to get a beautifully formatted report of your individual session.
2.  **Save to Library**: Click "Save to Library" to store the evaluation in your local browser database. This allows you to revisit it later.
3.  **Export CSV**: You can download a CSV file of your current session for external analysis.

## 4. Viewing Saved Samples
1.  **Go to Library**: Click the "Sample Library" link in the header.
2.  **Search & Filter**: Use the search bar to find samples by Code or Evaluator name.
3.  **Manage Samples**:
    *   **Delete**: Click the trash icon to remove a sample.
    *   **Select**: Click the checkbox on a card to select it for batch actions.

## 5. Comparing Samples
1.  **Select Samples**: In the Library, check the boxes for 2 or more samples you want to compare.
2.  **Click Compare**: The "Compare" button will become active. Click it to view a side-by-side visualization.
3.  **Analyze**: View the radar charts and bar graphs to see how the flavor profiles differ.

## 6. Data Import/Export (Backup)
*   **Export All**: In the Library, click the **Download (CSV)** button to backup all your saved samples to a single file.
*   **Import**: Use the **Upload** button to restore samples from a previously exported CSV file (e.g., when moving to a new device).

---
**Note**: This app works offline! Your data is stored locally in your browser. Clearing your browser cache may delete your saved samples, so remember to **Export All** regularly to keep a backup.
